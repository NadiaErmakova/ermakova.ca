Herring sale 2020

HERRING SALE is a GO on December 5th 2020.
Due to Covid-19, tickets will be sold on-line and the pick-up will be drive-thru.

Fishermen Helping Kids with Cancer ('FHKWC') is pleased to present the first on-line drive-thru herring sale. You won’t have to get out of your car - you drive, we’ll load the herring!
As usual, the BC fishing industry pays all costs for the herring sale - 100% of the money goes to kids being treated for cancer at BC Children's Hospital.

    PLEASE READ THE FOLLOWING CAREFULLY:
  • Ticket purchases will be on-line, and herring pick-up will be drive-thru.
  • $20 per bag. Each bag is approximately 20lbs.
  • Each one-hour time slot is a separate sale so picking up your fish will be fast.
  • If you can’t buy tickets for your desired time slot, please select another.
  • Check back if we are sold out because we may release more tickets for each time slot.
  • All sales are final. Tickets are non-transferable and non-refundable.
  • If the sale is delayed, your tickets can be used the following week.

Each one-hour time slot is a separate sale so pick the one works best for you:
8am - 9am 9am - 10am 10am - 11am 11am - 12pm 12pm - 1pm 1pm - 2pm 2pm - 3pm 3pm - 4pm

    LOCATION:
    We will be located in the same harbour, however, with the drive-thru, there are a few changes:
  • Drive south on Trites Road until Dyke Road. Turn left on Dyke Road and follow the signs to our site entrance on the right. Look for our friendly traffic crew to direct you.
  • Please ensure that you have your ticket ready - either printed or available on your phone for scanning.
  • Follow signs and our team members. Once you arrive in the pick-up area, present your ticket to the team member. Our crew will load the fish in the rear of your vehicle.
  • Prepare the truck or rear of your vehicle with a lined box for the bag(s) of herring. The bag(s) are sometimes wet.
  • Please remain in your vehicle at all times.
  • Drive slow and protect our team members.
  • Kindly wear a mask.
  • We will maintain social distancing and our ticket crew will be wearing masks.

WE HOPE WE'LL SEE YOU AT THE HERRING SALE- THANKS FOR HELPING TO MAKE IT A BIG SUCCESS!
