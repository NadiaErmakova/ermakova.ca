Be a Volunteer

We always have room for another volun- teer and there is a wide variety of jobs. If you can run a wheelbarrow to carry a few bags of herring, take tickets from customers, or welcome customers and volunteers with a smile, we have a job for you.

We are entirely run by volunteers, so you can find us at the dock on the day of the herring sale. The rest of the year, please feel free to contact us by email at fhkwcinfo@gmail.com
