Zare Shahbzayan

was born in 1977 in Yerevan, Armenia. Since 1984-1994 graduate from the Center for Arts Education, department of Ceramics of Ministry of Education of the RA. In 2000 - Graduated from the Yerevan State Academy of Art, the department of Sculpture. 2003 Got the First Prize of the XI International Biennale of Bronze Sculpture for "The cromlech time of Time" sculpture.
