About

Was born in 1977 in Yerevan, Armenia.

In 1994 graduated from the Armenian Center for Arts Education, department of Ceramics.

In 2000 graduated from Yerevan State Academy of Art.

In 2003 Got the First Prize of the XI International Biennale of Bronze Sculpture, for "The cromlech time of Time".

Zare has been a member of Artists' Union of RA since 2005.
