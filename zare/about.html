Zare Shahbzayan

Was born in 1977 in Yerevan, Armenia

1994 graduated from the Armenian Center for Arts Education, department of Ceramics.

In 2000 - Graduated from Yerevan State Academy of Art

2003 Got the First Prize of the XI International Biennale of Bronze Sculpture, for "The cromlech time of Time".

Has been a member of Artists' Union of RA since 2005.
