Embroidery

Embroidery is a great way to make memorable gifts, promote business/product identity or personalize garments such as shirts, t-shirts, towels, baby blankets, bath robes, linens, and any other personal items. For small businesses, there is no better way to distinguish your employees with business/product logo on uniforms or giving your customers tokens of appreciation that carry your image.

We offer custom monograms, lettering, and logo embroidery services with quality execution and attention to details in a convenient location with competitive pricing for the following:

Monograms/Lettering
We do embroidery of initials, individual names or lettering on t-shirts, bath robes, towels, socks, Christmas stockings, blankets, pillow cases, linens or any other personal items using any font style from our collection.

Digitized Logos
We do embroidery of business and corporate logos on uniforms, t-shirts, shirts, etc.

Non-Digitized Logos
We will help you bring your creative ideas to life by digitizing and embroidering logo artwork, pictures, or custom fonts using our experience in digitizing custom logos for embroidery. Our service includes matching logo elements shapes and layout, designing stitching textures for sewing on particular type of fabric, machine setup, sample production and finished embroidery on one garment or personal item.

Prices
Our pricing is based on letter size for lettering and stitch count for logo embroidery. Please, send us an inquiry or bring your item to our store to view samples and discuss options with our Customer Service.

Volume Discounts
Volume discounts available starting from 3 items for the same item type, size and embroidery option placement. Please contact us for more details.
