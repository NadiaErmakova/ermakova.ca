Company overview

For your convenience, we provide While You Wait*   service for all hems and small alterations while you shop at Guildford Town Shopping Centre.

Pro-Stitch is a professional clothing alterations, embroidery, garment repair and dry-cleaning services retail store located in Guildford Town Shopping Centre, Surrey, BC.
We have been serving local communities of Surrey, Langley, Coquitlam, Pitt Meadows and Maple Ridge in BC since 2000.
We are open 7 days a week during regular Guildford Town Centre shopping hours.

* While You Wait service for pants hemming and smaill repairs only. Conditions apply.

Loyalty Rewards

We truly appreciate your business and offer the following rewards program for our customers:

  • Collect a punch for each garment you bring for alteration!
  • Earn 10 punches to receive $5 reward towards next alteration.
  • Pickup the card at our store and start collecting punches today!

Terms and Conditions:

  • One punch per garment with a minimum $8 alteration.
  • Punches and discount are only applied at the time of payment.
  • There is no cash value for the punch or discount.
  • Valid only at Guildford location.

We help you save time and money
Clothing Alterations

Hemming, lengthening, shortening, tapering, restyling, labeling for garments such as jeans, pants, jackets, skirts, suits, leather, cocktail, wedding and prom dresses.
Embroidery

Monogramming, lettering, company/product logo embroidery on garments and personal items such as bath robes, towels, baby blankets, Christmas stockings. We work with small businesses and individuals.
Garment Repairs

Replacing zippers, patching holes, mending, sewing seams, fixing loose buttons, replacing buttons, etc.
Dry-cleaning

Drop off and pickup cleaned garments in one convenient location.
